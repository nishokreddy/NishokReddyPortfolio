01 / About

Securing What Matters

I'm an Associate Security Analyst embedded in Aurobindo Pharma's SOC, where I monitor, detect, and respond to threats across a regulated pharmaceutical enterprise — a high-stakes environment where security is non-negotiable.

My daily workflow revolves around SentinelOne AI SIEM, Stellar Cyber, Microsoft Sentinel, and SentinelOne EDR — triaging 60–100 alerts per day and turning raw signals into clear, actionable incident reports.

Beyond triage, I investigate malware infections, phishing , and unauthorized access attempts, correlate IOCs against threat feeds, and support ISO 27001 audit readiness. I also build Python-based tools to automate repetitive SOC tasks — because good analysts work smarter, not just harder.

I'm currently expanding my learnings into AI-driven security, and autonomous agents — the next frontier of defense operations and Cloud Security.
